PLANNING FOR A GROUP?

Group Rates are Valid for Any Group of Ten or More People: Adults (Ages 12-64) $1.75 Each Way or $3.50 Round Trip hildren (Ages 6-11) $1.00 Each Way or $1.75 Round Trip

Cash fares as well as Port Authority bus passes and tickets are accepted as payment. Seniors age 65 or older who present a blue or yellow PRT card or a Senior Connect Card ride free. Wheelchair accessibility is via the Upper Station located 1220 Grandview Avenue Pittsburgh, Pa. 15211.
